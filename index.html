DESIGNED & APPOINTED TO 5-STAR STANDARDS 按五星标准设计与配置

Located in Xiangqiao District, Chaozhou — a renowned historical and cultural city recognized as one of China's top tourist destinations — Yuyu Hotel opened in 2024 and is fully operated by Guangzhou White Swan Hotel Management. As one of China's most established 5-star hospitality groups, White Swan brings its mature service system and refined operational philosophy to every detail, ensuring guests enjoy a level of hospitality on par with international standards. The hotel's interiors are designed by the China Academy of Art, blending contemporary urban aesthetics with the cultural essence of Chaozhou's millennia-old heritage. From spatial layout to material selection, from craftsmanship to furnishings, every element is meticulously crafted to a five-star standard, offering both visual refinement and everyday comfort. Whether for a family getaway or a business trip, Yuyu Hotel delivers an elevated experience defined by quality, ease, and warmth in every stay.

潮州愉羽酒店位于中国广东省潮州市湘桥区。潮州市是中国历史名城、中国优秀旅游城市。酒店于2024年开业, 由广州白天鹅酒店管理公司全程运营管理。作为国内深具底蕴的五星级酒店管理集团,白天鹅以国际化五星标准为蓝本, 将成熟的服务体系与精细化运营理念注入其中,确保每一位宾客都能享受到媲美国际水准的尊崇礼遇。 酒店空间设计由中国美术学院担纲执笔,在都市现代美学的基调之上,巧妙融汇潮州本土的人文意蕴与在地精神, 令当代设计语言与千年古城气质相映成趣。从空间布局到材质遴选,从工艺细部到陈设配套,均严格遵循五星级标准精雕细琢, 兼具视觉品位与使用舒适。 无论是家庭亲子出游,还是商务差旅下榻,愉羽酒店都将以更高规格的品质体验,为每一次停留赋予从容与温度。

ROOM TYPES

Yuyu Hotel features 158 individually styled guestrooms, including Deluxe Twin Rooms, Deluxe King Rooms, Family Rooms and Executive Suites, catering to a wide range of traveler needs. All rooms are non-smoking, bright and spacious, each with its own window for abundant natural light and open views. Standard rooms measure approximately 35 square meters, offering a comfortable and well-proportioned layout. Rooms are equipped with smart controls for lighting, temperature and curtains, and bedding is changed for every guest — soft, skin-friendly linens that ensure a clean and restful night's sleep.

Here, every bite unfolds the rich culinary heritage of Chaozhou — proudly known as China's "City of Gastronomy."

Culture & Cuisine文旅与美食

CULTURE & CUISINE

Chaozhou is a nationally recognized historical and cultural city, celebrated as one of China's top tourist destinations, a renowned culinary capital, and a hub of ceramic craftsmanship, with a rich cultural heritage and abundant tourism resources. Chaozhou cuisine is famous far and wide — beef balls, braised goose and oyster omelets are dishes travelers remember long after their visit. Local specialties such as Phoenix Dancong tea, tofu-milk pastries and the Three Treasures of Chaozhou are also celebrated both at home and abroad as beloved souvenirs.

潮州是国家历史文化名城,也是中国优秀旅游城市、美食之都、陶瓷之都,人文底蕴深厚,旅游资源丰富。 潮州菜远近闻名,牛肉丸、卤鹅、蚝仔烙等经典美食令人回味无穷。此外,凤凰单枞茶、腐乳饼、潮州三宝等特产 也享誉海内外,是备受青睐的手信佳品。
